Florilegium

Florilegium is a self-defined space that fixes a real-world problem. It tackles a niche group of people: the average reader who wants to take part in the online community. This is a website for readers who just want to track their reading online and interact with friend's reading and also for readers who want to interact with the Online Book Community. This ideation is the first step of an ongoing passion project.
